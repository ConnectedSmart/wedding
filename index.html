Our Story

He knew her as "Banana Girl." She knew him as that guy who loves animal crackers--and as fate or fortune may have it, they began falling in love on that first weekend before school began junior year at Virginia Tech. She took him to the New River, blue dress and bare feet, and they spent time sitting by the river bank, just talking. He took her to her first small production film in Roanoke and won her over because she wanted to try the Bojangles macaroni and cheese.

They've been inseparable ever since (even through two years of long distance), and after almost four years of hiking up mountains and watching indie flicks and having lots of fun along the way, Sam asked Lauren to marry him on a very hot but extremely special day in Ocean City, New Jersey.
